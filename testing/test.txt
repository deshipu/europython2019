1. Insert two AAA batteries and switch the switch to the ON position.
2. The screen should look like "before.jpg".
3. Verify that all pixels are shining.
4. Press each of the 6 buttons.
5. The screen should look like "after.jpg".
6. Connect USB cable to a computer.
7. A CIRCUITPY disk should appear, open it.
8. Delete the "code.py" file.
9. Safely remove the CIRCUITPY disk.
10. There should be scrolling text on the screen.
11. Switch the switch to the OFF position.
12. Remove the batteries.
